Tuna Salad

Ingredients:

  • 5-6 leaves of lettuce
  • 2-3 sprigs of parsley
  • 4 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 can of small tuna
  • 2 tablespoons canned corn
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Lettuce, parsley, and tomatoes are washed well and chopped.
  2. Chopped ingredients are placed in a suitable bowl.
  3. Add drained tuna and corn. You can buy canned corn from the market, or you can boil it yourself at home.
  4. Add olive oil, salt, and lemon.
  5. All ingredients are mixed well and served.
  6. Your Tuna Salad is ready. Enjoy your meal!
