Fit Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons gelatin
  • 1 tortilla bread or whole grain lavash
  • 70 grams of boiled chicken (breast)
  • 30 grams of grated cheddar (can be light)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste (pepper or tomato, optional)
  • Desired spices (thyme, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the bottom of the pan and place tortilla bread on it.
  2. Place tomato paste, chicken and cheddar cheese on top.
  3. Add desired spices.
  4. Cover the pan and wait for it to cook for about 7 minutes.
  5. Your fit pizza is ready. Enjoy your meal!
